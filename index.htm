About

Why Cart Monkey

Cart Monkey's exceptional customer service and factory directory pricing is a win for everyone.

  • Exceptional customer service.
  • Full knowledge of the industry.
  • Factory direct pricing.

We are proud to offer ONLY the industry leading Club Car Precendent.

What sets the Precendent golf cars apart?

  • The industry's only rust proof aluminum frame for durability and long life.
  • Independent suspension, an automotive-oriented chassis and a sports-car-like driving experience.
  • The IQ electrical system that captures and store energy lost during braking and increases range.
  • Classic stlying, unsurpassed comfort, and lots of accessories and options.
